About Ruby Howes

With a passion for creativity and beauty, she brings a fresh and innovative approach to hairstyling. Ruby's expertise lies in creating modern and stylish haircuts that suit each client's unique personality and preferences. Her dedication to staying updated on the latest trends ensures that her clients always leave her salon feeling confident and satisfied. Beyond her exceptional skills, Ruby is known for her warm and welcoming personality, making every appointment a positive and enjoyable experience.
